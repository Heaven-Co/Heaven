FAQ

What is Heaven?
+
Heaven is a vibrant gaming community where players come together to enjoy their favorite games, make new friends, and relax. Whether you're into competitive gaming, casual play, or just want a place to hang out and chat, Heaven provides the perfect environment for gamers of all types to connect and have fun.
How do I join the Heaven community?
+
Joining is super easy! Just click the Discord logo button in the top right corner of the page. This will take you directly to our Discord server where you can start your Heaven journey immediately!
What games do you play in Heaven?
+
We play everything! Whether you're into FPS games, RPGs, MOBAs, battle royales, or indie titles, there's something for everyone. Head to our "find-players" channel, mention the game you want to play, and you'll quickly meet community members who share your gaming interests. From AAA titles to hidden gems, our diverse community covers it all!
How do I get verified in the server?
+
Getting verified is simple! When you join the server, head over to the "verify" channel. Our verification bot has clear instructions explaining the entire process step-by-step. Just follow the prompts, and you'll be verified in no time. This helps us keep the community safe and secure for everyone!
Are there age restrictions?
+
Yes, Heaven is a 15+ community. All members must be at least 15 years old to join and participate. This helps us maintain an appropriate environment and ensure conversations and content are suitable for our community members.
What kind of events do you host?
+
Heaven hosts a wide variety of exciting events! From competitive gaming tournaments and custom game nights to community movie sessions, giveaways, and special seasonal celebrations. We also organize voice chat hangouts, creative contests, trivia nights, and collaborative gaming sessions. There's always something fun happening, so keep an eye on our events channel to never miss out!
How can I become a moderator or staff member?
+
We love dedicated community members! To be considered for a staff position, you should be actively participating in Heaven for more than a week. Once you've established yourself as a positive member of the community, open a ticket and submit your staff application request. Our team will review your activity, contributions, and fit for the role.
What are the server rules?
+
All server rules are clearly outlined in our dedicated "rules" channel. When you join Heaven, make sure to read through them carefully to understand our community guidelines, what's expected from members, and what behavior is not tolerated. Following these rules helps keep Heaven a fun, safe, and welcoming space for everyone!
How do I report a problem or contact support?
+
If you encounter any issues, have concerns, or need assistance, simply open a ticket through our ticketing system. Once you create a ticket, our staff team will move you to a private support channel where you can discuss any problems, ask questions, or share feedback. We're here to help and want to ensure you have the best experience possible in Heaven!
